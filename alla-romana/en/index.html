Alla Romana

Expense splitting among friends, flatmates and travel companions. Add an expense, pick who paid and how to split it — equally, exact amounts, percentages or shares — and the app works out who owes what to whom with the fewest payments.

Your data lives in your own iCloud: no account to create, no server of ours. Shared groups sync across your devices and with the other members through iCloud.
